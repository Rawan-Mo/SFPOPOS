CONCRETE JUNGLE

A DEEP DIVE INTO SAN FRANCISCO's DOWNTOWN

Walking through San Francisco’s Financial District, you might be surprised to find a small grove of redwoods, right next to one of the most iconic buildings in SF, the 48-story Transamerica Pyramid. It’s both magical and unnatural, the awe-inspiring trees contrasted by sleek futurist architecture. At nearly any time of day you can catch people sitting and eating, chatting with a friend or even walking through with a dog. But make no mistake, this isn’t one of San Francisco’s famous parks. What you’re experiencing is one of San Francisco’s first POPOS.
